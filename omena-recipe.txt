# Omena Preparation Protocol

**Ingredients:**

* 250g dried omena
* Warm water (for washing)
* 3-4 tbsp cooking oil
* 1 large red onion, finely chopped
* 3 medium tomatoes, blended or diced
* 1 tbsp minced garlic and ginger
* 1 small bunch fresh coriander
* Salt and spices to taste



**Steps:**
1. Purify: Sort the omena to remove debris. Wash in warm water, rubbing gently, and repeat 2–3 times until the water is clear; drain well.

2. Crisp: Heat oil in a pan and fry the drained omena for 5–8 minutes until golden-brown and crispy, then set aside.

3. Aromatics: Using the same pan, sauté the onions until translucent. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for 30 seconds.

4. Stew: Stir in the tomatoes, salt, and spices. Simmer for 5 minutes until a thick paste forms (add water if needed for consistency).

5. Combine: Add the crispy omena to the tomato base and simmer for 3–4 minutes to absorb flavors. Turn off the heat and fold in the fresh coriander.
