Recipe: Garlic Shrimp Butter Pasta
By: David Munene

Servings: 2
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

INGREDIENTS:
- 200g spaghetti or linguine
- 250g shrimp (peeled and deveined)
- 4 tbsp butter
- 5 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
- 1/2 cup pasta water
- Salt to taste
- Black pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)
- 1/2 lemon (juice and zest)
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Cook the Pasta:
   - Boil salted water in a pot.
   - Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
   - Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.

2. Cook the Shrimp:
   - Heat olive oil and 2 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat.
   - Add shrimp, season with salt and pepper.
   - Cook for 2–3 minutes per side until pink and opaque.
   - Remove shrimp and set aside.

3. Make the Garlic Butter Sauce:
   - In the same pan, add remaining butter.
   - Add minced garlic and chili flakes.
   - Sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant (do not burn garlic).

4. Combine Everything:
   - Add cooked pasta into the pan.
   - Pour in reserved pasta water little by little to create sauce.
   - Add shrimp back in and mix well.

5. Finish:
   - Add lemon juice, parsley, and parmesan cheese.
   - Toss everything until evenly coated.

6. Serve hot and enjoy
