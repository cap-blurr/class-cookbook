Egg Curry

Ingredients:
- 4 boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika or chili powder
- 1 cup water
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander 

Instructions:
1. Boil the eggs, peel them, and set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the onions until golden brown.
3. Add garlic and ginger, then cook for 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften into a paste.
5. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, paprika, and salt.
6. Add water and simmer for 5 minutes to form a curry sauce.
7. Add the boiled eggs and gently coat them with the sauce.
8. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Serving Suggestion:
Serve with rice, chapati, naan, or ugali.
